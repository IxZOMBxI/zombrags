ABOUT ZOMB RAGS

Survival isn’t just a theme — it’s the code we live by.

Zomb Rags is a fusion of cyberpunk aesthetics, survival-grade durability, and bespoke craft. We don’t follow trends — we design for the ones who’ve been through hell, taken the hits, and kept moving. Every piece is built to outlast, both in style and in function.

The brand was born from the idea that clothing and accessories should do more than just look good — they should tell a story. A story of grit, resilience, and never backing down. Each drop is limited, making every item a rare artifact in a world of disposable fashion.

The Mission

To create high-end, functional techwear and accessories that merge utility with art — a visual language for the modern scavenger.

What Makes Us Different

  • Bespoke Design: Each piece is tailored for durability, style, and function.
  • Limited Drops: No mass production. No endless restocks. When it’s gone, it’s gone.
  • Raw Process: We show the behind-the-scenes — unfiltered, unpolished, real.

The Horde

If you’ve made it here, you’re already one of us. The Horde is more than a community — it’s a network of survivors, makers, and rebels who refuse to fade into the noise.

Stay sharp. Stay moving. And remember — we see you.
